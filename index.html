Wine Tour Guide

What to expect on your Wine Tour

Whether you've already booked or you're thinking about visiting, here's everything you need to know before your tour.

Before You Arrive

Tours run in most weather conditions, so comfortable shoes and weather appropriate clothing are all you need.

  • Arrive 10 to 15 minutes early
  • No voucher or email required
  • Avoid strong perfume or aftershave

On The Tour

Explore the vineyard and winery, discover how English wine is made and enjoy a guided tasting.

  • Tour lasts around 1.5 to 2 hours
  • Vineyard and winery included
  • Adults only experience

After Your Tour

There's no rush to leave. Stay for a drink, food or browse the Wine Shop after your tasting.

  • Wine Bar and Shop on site
  • Cars can be left overnight
  • Taxis should be booked in advance

Getting Here

Just a short walk from Twyford Station, with easy access from Reading and London.

  • Elizabeth Line and National Rail
  • Around 15 minutes on foot
  • Free parking available
Useful Information
👕

What should I wear?

Comfortable shoes and weather appropriate clothing are all you need. If it's raining, bring a waterproof coat or umbrella.

🌧️

Bad weather?

Tours run in most weather conditions, so a little rain won't stop us.

👃

Perfume & Aftershave

Wine tasting is largely about aroma, so we recommend avoiding strong fragrances.

🚕

Need a taxi?

Please book in advance. Uber is often unavailable locally and cars cannot enter the estate after 8pm.

The estate

One of England’s established vineyard stories.

Vines were first planted here in 1979. Today, visitors can explore the vineyard, winery, Wine Bar and historic estate setting in one place.
