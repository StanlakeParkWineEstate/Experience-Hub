Around Stanlake Park

Turn the visit into a day out.

Use these ideas before booking, after booking or while you’re already here.

Food and drink lovers

Pair the Wine Bar with local breweries, independent coffee, good pubs and Thames Valley restaurants.

Walks and fresh air

Build in a gentle local walk before or after your tasting, especially if you’re making a full day of it.

Nearby towns

Explore Twyford for convenience, Reading for shopping and culture, Henley for the river, and Marlow or Windsor for a bigger day out.

Rainy day ideas

Use indoor tastings, the Wine Bar, Reading shopping, escape rooms, museums and cosy pubs as your backup plan.
